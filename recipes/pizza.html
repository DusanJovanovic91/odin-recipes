Tortilla Pizza Recipe

How to make Tortilla Pizza

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Tortilla Pizza Ingredients

  • Tortilla: Use store-bought or homemade soft flour tortillas.
  • Oil: Brush the tortillas with olive oil before you season them.
  • Seasoning: These tortilla pizzas are seasoned with garlic powder, black pepper, and dried oregano.
  • Tomato sauce: Use store-bought or homemade tomato sauce.
  • Toppings: Top the tortilla pizzas with sliced chicken breast, green bell pepper, and green oninons. Of course, you can customize the toppings to suit your taste.
  • Cheese: Finish the pizzas with mozzarella cheese.

How to Make Pizza with a Tortilla

Here's brief overwiev of what you can expect when you make easy tortilla pizzas:

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive oil, season, and bake.
  2. Top with tomato sauce and toppings. Sprinkle it with cheese.
  3. Bake again until cheese is melted, then season it with oregano.
